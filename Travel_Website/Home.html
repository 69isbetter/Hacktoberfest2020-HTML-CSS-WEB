TRAVEL TO THE CLIFFS IN NEGRIL

It is a small boutique resort that is not on the beach, but on the cliffs overlooking the Caribbean Sea. The Caves is different and quiet and low key and perfect. Four of us went the first week of December, which I will have to remind myself in the future is ideal weather in Jamaica – mid to high 80’s, lots of sunshine and warm water.

YANGON, MYANMAR

Yangon (formerly Rangoon) is filled with many colonial buildings that have fallen into disrepair, and more modern buildings that also need some TLC. It does have an extraordinary site, the Shwedagon Pagoda. It is the most sacred Buddhist pagoda in the country, and it is truly amazing. The giant gold primary stupa (said to hold some hairs from the Buddha’s head) is surrounded by countless smaller stupas and large pavilions.
